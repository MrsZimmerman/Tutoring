About Me

I grew up in Exeter Township, where I still live today. I am currently a high school computer science teacher in Lancaster County, and I have been passionate about helping others learn for as long as I can remember.

My tutoring journey began in 10th grade, when I started helping classmates through my school’s guidance office. That experience sparked a lifelong love for providing one-on-one learning support.

I have had the opportunity to work with a wide range of learners: from elementary students in an after-school program, to high school students I tutored weekly, to adults seeking to build confidence with computers and new technology.

Since graduating college, I have continued tutoring high school students in various areas of mathematics, taught multiple summer camps, and provided personalized tech coaching for adults. Whether it’s a one-time session or ongoing support, I am always excited to meet learners where they are and help them grow.
